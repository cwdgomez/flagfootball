Photo & Media Consent Form
Youth Athlete Image Release · ITCC LLC
This form must be completed by a parent or legal guardian.
1. Purpose of This Form

CGMax FFTP — Flag Football Tracker Pro allows coaches to associate a player photo with each athlete's profile within the app. This form authorizes the collection and use of your child's photograph for the purposes described below.

By signing this form, you provide informed consent as required under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable state privacy laws.

2. How Photos Are Used

Your child's photo will only be used for:

  • Displaying the player's profile image within the CGMax FFTP coaching app on the coach's personal device
  • Appearing on digitally generated game scorecards and stat summaries viewable only by the coaching staff

Photos will NOT be: uploaded to any server, shared on social media, used for advertising or marketing, sold to third parties, or made publicly accessible in any way. All images are stored locally on the coach's device using browser storage and are never transmitted over the internet.

3. Data Storage & Security

Player photos are stored exclusively in the web browser's local storage on the coach's personal device. Photos are automatically resized to a small thumbnail (120×120 pixels) before storage. No cloud servers, databases, or third-party services have access to these images.

The coach is responsible for maintaining the physical security of their device. Photos can be deleted at any time by removing the player from the roster within the app or by clearing the browser's local storage.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

CGMax FFTP does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. This form serves as that verifiable consent. By signing below, you confirm that:

  • You are the parent or legal guardian of the named child
  • You understand how the photo will be used and stored
  • You have the legal authority to provide this consent
  • The child is a participant on the team identified in this form
5. Your Rights

You may withdraw this consent at any time by notifying the coach verbally or in writing. Upon withdrawal, the coach will promptly delete your child's photo from the app. You also have the right to:

  • Request to see the photo currently stored in the app
  • Request immediate deletion of the photo at any time
  • Decline to sign this form — your child may still fully participate on the team without a profile photo

This consent is valid for the current athletic season only and must be renewed for subsequent seasons.
