Live Parent Scoreboard

The parent stuck at work. The grandparent three states away. The sibling away at college. They all follow every TD, every flag pull, every quarter — live, on their phone, right now.

Coach generates a link before the game and drops it in the team group chat. Anyone who can't make it taps it and watches the score and stats update in real time. No app install, no account, no setup — just open and follow along.

1
Coach taps "Go Live Now" in the Live Sync panel — a 6-character game code is generated instantly
2
Tap "Copy Parent Link" and paste it into the team group chat — parents tap it on any browser, no install needed
3
Stats appear live as the coach logs every TD, flag pull, and catch — tap "End Live" when the game is over

Stat Coach — Co-Coaching

Hand a second phone to your assistant coach or a parent volunteer. They log stats from their own device and everything syncs to yours in real time — no shared screen, no shouting across the field.

1
Tap "Stat Coach" in the Live Sync panel — a unique game link is created for your assistant
2
Share the link with your co-coach — they open it in any browser, no app install or account needed
3
Both coaches log stats simultaneously — touchdowns, catches, flag pulls, and more merge in real time on both devices
