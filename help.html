Voice Command Guide
Say the player's name + the stat.
The app listens and logs it instantly.
💡 Works best on Chrome (Android) and Safari (iOS 17+). Speak clearly in a normal voice — no need to shout. A ding sound means it was recorded. A buzz means try again.
📊 Logging Player Stats
⭐
Say: [Name] touchdown or [Name] scored or [Name] TD
"Jaxon touchdown" "Reed scored" "Phoenix TD"
🏈
Say: [Name] caught it or [Name] reception or [Name] catch
"Cade caught it" "Milo reception" "Nico catch"
🏃
Say: [Name] flag pull or [Name] pulled or [Name] flag
"Grayson flag pull" "Jax pulled" "Cade flag"
🎯
Say: [Name] interception or [Name] intercepted or [Name] pick six
"Reed interception" "Nico intercepted" "Phoenix pick six"
For defensive interceptions only. QB throwing interceptions use QB-specific commands below.
🛡️
Say: [Name] blocked or [Name] block
"Jaxon blocked" "Milo block"
💨
Say: [Name] fumbled or [Name] fumble or [Name] dropped
"Cade fumbled" "Jax dropped"
🔄
Say: [Name] conversion or [Name] one point or [Name] two point
"Reed conversion" "Nico two point" "Jaxon one point"
⚡ QB-Specific Stats
⚡
Say: [QB name] TD pass or [QB name] touchdown pass or [QB name] threw a TD
"Grayson TD pass" "Grayson touchdown pass" "Grayson threw a TD"
The player set as QB in your roster will automatically receive QB stats.
❌
Say: [QB name] threw interception or [QB name] picked off
"Grayson threw interception" "Grayson picked off" "Grayson interception thrown"
🔄
Say: [QB name] conversion pass or [QB name] two point pass
"Grayson conversion pass" "Grayson two point pass"
🔄 Setting the Lineup
⚡
Say: Offense is [player names]
"Offense is Jaxon Reed Cade Milo Nico Phoenix" "Put Jaxon Reed on offense"
List up to 6 players by first name. The current offense will be cleared and replaced.
🛡
Say: Defense is [player names]
"Defense is Jaxon Reed Cade Milo Nico Phoenix" "Defense team Jaxon Reed Cade"
🗑
Say: Clear offense or Clear defense or Reset field
"Clear offense" "Clear defense" "Reset field"
Removes all players from that side and logs their snaps.
🏆 Score Commands
✅
Say: We scored or Our team scored
"We scored" "Our team scored" "Us scored"
Adds 6 points (TD) to your team's score.
❌
Say: They scored or Opponent scored
"They scored" "Opponent scored" "Opponent touchdown"
💡 Tips for Best Results
🎙️
Say the name first, then the stat. "Jaxon touchdown" works better than "touchdown Jaxon".
🔊
Listen for the ding (success) or buzz (not recognized). The buzz means try again with different words.
👥
Use the first name from your roster (e.g. "Jaxon" not "Jaxon B."). The app matches partial names.
📶
Voice recognition requires internet to process audio. It will not work offline.
📱
iOS tip: Tap "Start Listening", speak clearly, then tap "Stop Listening". Safari handles this best in short bursts.
🔄
Auto-correct: If a non-QB player says "TD pass", the app automatically logs it as a regular TD for them.
