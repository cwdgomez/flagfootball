Help & Guide
Everything you need to coach smarter on game day — setup, scoring, voice, and all Pro features.
⚙️
Game Setup
Getting ready for kickoff
1
Enter Your Team & Opponent
Type your team name and your opponent's name. Choose the number of quarters (1–4). Your team name and colors are saved automatically.
2
Build Your Roster
Tap Edit Roster to add players (first name + last initial). Assign jersey numbers. Set your QB. Your roster persists across all games.
3
Mark Attendance
Tap any player in the Absent list to mark them out. Absent players are excluded from rotations and don't appear in stats.
4
Pick Your Colors
Choose a primary and secondary color for your scoreboard, buttons, and TD animations. Tap any color swatch to apply it.
5
⚡ Kick Off
Tap KICK OFF to enter the game screen. Everything auto-saves as you play — no data is lost if you switch apps mid-game.
✅
Auto-save is on the whole time. If you get a call or switch apps, your game state is automatically restored when you come back. Just open the app again.
🏈
Scoring
Track the game score
+TD
+TD Button
Adds 6 points. Triggers a touchdown fanfare (ascending musical tones), haptic buzz, and score animation. Works for both teams.
+1
+1pt / +2pt
Use these for conversion attempts after a touchdown. Tap +1 for a 1-point conversion run/pass, +2 for a 2-point conversion.
↩
Undo Score
The ↩ button removes 1 point from that team's current quarter score. Tap it immediately after a mis-tap.
Q1
Quarter Bar
Shows the score for each quarter. Tap Next Q → in the header to advance to the next quarter. The 🏁 Final button ends the game.
⏱
Quarter Timer
Tap the timer display to set a countdown. Tap ▶ to start/pause it. The timer alerts you at halftime and when the game ends.
📊
Player Stats
Logging game stats per player
🏃
Tapping Stat Buttons
Each player card has stat buttons. Tap once to add 1. The button flashes to confirm. Stats are broken down by quarter automatically.
🔤
Available Stats
All players: Catches, Flag Pulls, TDs, INTs, Conversions, Fumbles, Blocks
QB only: TD Passes, INTs Thrown, Conv. Passes
🏆
MVP Score
Each player's stat points feed a live MVP formula. The podium (🥇🥈🎖️) updates in real time in the Summary tab.
📝
Coach Notes
Tap a player's name on the stats screen to expand their card and add a private note — great for post-game feedback.
🔄
Rotations
Track who's on offense and defense
⚡
Setting the Offense / Defense
Tap a player's name in the Rotations tab to toggle them ON OFFENSE (green) or ON DEFENSE (blue). Tapping again removes them from the field.
📈
Snap Counts
Every time a player is rotated off the field, their snap count increments. See cumulative offensive and defensive snaps in the Summary tab → Play Time chart.
🤫
Why Rotations Matter
Use the data at end-of-season to make sure every player gets fair field time — especially important in youth leagues where equal play is required.
🚩
Penalty Tracker
Live flag counts for both teams
📍
The Penalty Tracker bar appears automatically below the scoreboard on every game screen — no setup required.
+🚩
Adding a Penalty Flag
Tap the +🚩 button on the appropriate side (your team or opponent) every time a flag is thrown. The count updates instantly.
↩
Undo a Flag
Tapped by mistake? Hit ↩ to remove the last flag from that team's count.
📋
In the Summary
If any penalties were called, a 🚩 Penalties row appears in the Game Summary tab showing the final flag count for both teams.

Hands-free coaching on the sideline
💡
Works best on Chrome (Android) and Safari (iOS 17+). Tap the 🎙️ Voice Log button, speak clearly, and the app logs the stat instantly. A ding = logged. A buzz = try again.

🔄 Lineup & Score Commands

🛡
Say: Defense is [player names]

Say: Clear offense or Clear defense or Reset field
✅

❌

💡 Voice Tips
🎙️
Say the name first, then the stat — "Jaxon touchdown" not "touchdown Jaxon".
🔊
Ding = logged. Buzz = not recognized. If you hear a buzz, rephrase and try again.
👥
Use first name only (e.g. "Jaxon" not "Jaxon B."). The app matches partial names.
📶
Voice recognition requires internet to process audio. It won't work offline.
📱
iOS tip: Tap "Start Listening", speak, then tap "Stop Listening". Short bursts work best.

Tournament ModePRO
Bracket management for league events
🔗
Access Tournament Mode at cgmaxfftp.com/tournament.html — or tap the Tournament link in the app.
1
Create a Tournament
Choose your format: Single Elimination or Round Robin. Select 4, 8, or a custom number of teams (up to 16).
2
Enter Team Names
Type in each team name. The bracket auto-generates with the correct seeding structure.
3
Enter Game Scores

4
Live Game Link
Tap 🏈 Start Live Game on any matchup to jump straight into the main app for that matchup — stats and rotations ready to go.
🥇
Champion Display
When the final game is scored, the champion team is displayed with a 🏆 trophy and confetti effect. Tournament is saved to history automatically.
📋
Tournament History

Play DesignerPRO
Draw and save your playbook
🔗
Access Play Designer at cgmaxfftp.com/playdesigner.html — or tap the Play Designer link in the app.
🟢
The Field
A dark green canvas field with yard lines and a line of scrimmage. Pinch to zoom on mobile for precision drawing.
👤
Players
6 offensive players (C, QB, WR1–WR4) with default formation positions. Toggle defensive players on/off. Drag any player to reposition them.
🖊️
Drawing Routes
Switch to Draw Routes mode. Tap a player to select them (their color highlights), then draw their route on the field. Each player gets their own color.
💾
Saving Plays
Tap Save Play, name your play, and it's added to your playbook. Up to 20 plays saved locally with miniature canvas thumbnails.
↩
Undo
Tap Undo Route to erase the last route you drew. Reset the field to default positions with the Reset button.

Live Parent ScoreboardPRO
Real-time score link for parents
1
Start a Live Session
In the app, find the 📱 Parent Link section (below the scoreboard). Tap GO LIVE NOW — a 6-character session code is generated.
2

Stat Coach — Co-CoachingPRO
Log stats from a second device in real time
🤝
Stat Coach lets you hand off stat logging to an assistant coach on their own phone. They see your full roster, tap stat buttons, and the stats appear instantly on your primary device — no shared screen, no delay.
1
Go Live First
Tap the 📡 sync button in the game screen header and tap GO LIVE NOW. The Live Parent Scoreboard must be active to use Stat Coach — both features share the same session.
2
Copy the Stat Coach Link
In the sync modal, a purple 🤝 STAT COACH LINK section appears below the parent link. Tap Copy Stat Coach Link and text it to your assistant coach.
3
Assistant Opens on Their Phone
Your assistant taps the link on their phone. statcoach.html opens, shows your full roster with their on-field status (Offense / Defense / Bench), and connects to the live session automatically.
4
Tap Stats — They Appear Instantly
When the assistant taps a stat button (Catch, TD, Flag Pull, etc.), it broadcasts to your device and shows a 🤝 toast notification confirming the stat was logged. Stats are saved exactly as if you tapped them yourself.
👤
Regular Player Buttons
Catch · TD · Flag Pull · INT · Fumble · Deflection
🏈
QB Player Buttons
TD Pass · INT Thrown · Conv Pass · Catch · Fumble · Deflection — QB is auto-detected from your roster settings.
🔄
Roster Updates Automatically
Any time you change a rotation, score a TD, or advance a quarter on the primary device, the Stat Coach screen updates automatically — your assistant always sees the current state.
⚡
No Account Needed for the Assistant
The assistant coach doesn't need a Pro license or account — just the link. Only the primary coach (the one who went live) needs Pro.
💡
Tip: Send the Stat Coach link before the game starts. Your assistant can connect and do a test tap during warm-ups to confirm the connection is working before kickoff.

Sign In & Cloud SyncPRO
Your data on every device, automatically
☁️
Signing in is optional — you can use your Pro license key and all local features without ever creating an account. But signing in gives you automatic cross-device sync so your roster and season data follows you everywhere.
1
Find the Sign-In Button
On the app setup screen, look for the 👤 Signed In strip or a sign-in option in the header. You can sign in with Google, Apple, or a magic link email sent to your inbox.
2
Choose Your Sign-In Method
Google — tap and sign in via Google's secure OAuth popup. Apple — uses Apple ID with Face ID / Touch ID. Email link — enter your email and tap the magic link sent to you; no password needed.
3
Your Data Syncs Automatically
Once signed in, your roster, season history, team colors, and settings sync silently in the background whenever you save a game or make changes. Open the app on a second phone and sign in — everything is already there.
4
Sign Out
On the setup screen, the 👤 Signed In strip shows your email and a Sign Out button. Tapping Sign Out switches back to local-only mode — your existing local data is unaffected.
🔒
Your Data is Private
Cloud sync is protected by Row Level Security — only you can access your own data. ITCC LLC never reads, shares, or sells your coaching data.
📱
Works on Multiple Devices
Sign in on your iPad at home and your phone at the field — both stay in sync automatically. Your season data is always up to date.

Cloud Backup & RestorePRO
Save your season data to the cloud
1
Open the Season Screen
Tap 📚 Season from the game summary or setup screen to view your season history and data actions.
2
Tap ☁️ Backup

3
Restore on Any Device
Tap ☁️ Restore on a new phone or after clearing your browser. Enter the same license key and your full season history is pulled back down.
💡
Tip: Back up after every game day. If you switch phones, install the app on your new device, enter your Pro key, and restore — all your season data comes with you.

Coach Tips
Get the most out of game day
📴
Install on your home screen (Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen) so it works fully offline with no browser chrome. This is the best way to use it.
🔋
Keep your screen on. On iOS, go to Settings → Display → Auto-Lock → Never during game day. The timer still works if the screen dims.
🎙️
Voice logging beats tapping when you're moving around the sideline. Set up your roster names ahead of time so the app can match them.
📱
Send the parent link before kickoff. Tap GO LIVE NOW while you're still warming up so parents have the link ready when the game starts.
💾
Save every game to build your season record. End-of-season trends are only as good as the data you save game by game.
🔄
Use the Undo buttons liberally. Every scoreboard button and most stat buttons have an undo — no need to fear mis-taps.
🚩
Track penalties from the start. By end-of-season you'll see which games had the most flags — useful for identifying discipline patterns.
☀️
Use Light Mode outdoors. Tap ☀️ in the header to switch to a high-contrast light theme that's easier to read in bright sunlight.
