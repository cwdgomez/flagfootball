Hey Coach,
You just unlocked the full coaching experience. Your Pro license is active and every feature is ready to go. Here's a quick guide to the three features coaches love most.
🎙️
#1 — VOICE STAT LOGGING
Tap the 🎙️ microphone button during a game and say commands like "Jaxon touchdown" or "Reed flag pull". The app logs the stat hands-free so you never have to look down at your phone. You'll hear a confirmation tone when the stat is recorded and an error beep if it doesn't match — just try again.
Tip: Use first names that match your roster exactly. Set up your roster names before game day for the best accuracy.
🔐
#2 — GOOGLE / APPLE SIGN-IN & CLOUD SYNC
On the app setup screen, tap the Sign In option and choose Google, Apple, or Email Link. Once signed in, your roster, season history, and team settings sync automatically to every device you use — no manual backup needed. Sign in on your iPad at home and your phone at the field and both stay in sync.
Tip: Sign in right now before your first game. Your data will be safe even if you switch phones or clear your browser.
🤝
#3 — STAT COACH (CO-COACHING)
Go Live, then look for the purple 🤝 Stat Coach Link section in the sync panel. Copy the link and text it to your assistant coach. They open it on their phone and can log stats from the full roster — no Pro account needed on their end. Every stat they tap appears on your screen instantly.
Tip: Great for when you're focused on the offense side and need someone else tracking defense stats at the same time.
📡
#4 — LIVE PARENT SCOREBOARD
During a game, tap the 📡 sync button in the header and tap GO LIVE NOW. A shareable link is created instantly — copy it and paste it into your team group chat. Parents open it in any browser (no app install needed) and see the score update in real-time as you coach.
Tip: Send the link to parents during warm-ups so they're ready before kickoff. The parent view is read-only and auto-expires after 12 hours.
📚
#5 — SEASON HISTORY + CLOUD BACKUP
After every game, tap 💾 End Game & Save to Season. Your win/loss record, player leaderboard, MVP awards, and individual player stats build up over the entire season. Use 📋 Summary for a beautiful end-of-season report, or ☁️ Backup to save everything to the cloud.
Tip: Back up after every game day. If you switch phones, just enter your license key on the new device and tap ☁️ Restore.
